| SAIYAN SAGA |

The Saiyan Saga begins when Raditz, Goku's brother, arrives on Earth and reveals Goku's Saiyan origins, announcing the arrival of two powerful warriors, Vegeta and Nappa. After Goku sacrifices himself to help Piccolo defeat Raditz, the Z Fighters prepare intensely for the coming battle. Goku trains in the Other World with King Kai, while his friends train Gohan. When Vegeta and Nappa land on the planet, they engage in fierce battles that result in the deaths of many fighters, including Yamcha, Tien, Chiaotzu, and Piccolo. | FRIEZA SAGA |

The Frieza Saga follows the Z Fighters as they travel to Planet Namek to find the Namekian Dragon Balls and resurrect their fallen friends after the battles with the Saiyans. Upon arrival, they discover that Frieza, a ruthless galactic tyrant, is already there collecting the Dragon Balls to gain immortality. Vegeta, also pursuing the Dragon Balls, forges uneasy alliances and rivalries with Gohan, Krillin, and eventually Piccolo, who joins the fight after fusing with Nail. As Frieza's elite forces are defeated, the heroes confront Frieza himself, whose power escalates through multiple terrifying transformations. After witnessing the brutal deaths of Vegeta and Krillin, Goku is driven into a rage that triggers his legendary transformation into a Super Saiyan. In a climactic battle, Goku ultimately defeats Frieza, who tries to destroy Namek in his final act of spite. The surviving heroes escape as Planet Namek explodes, and Goku barely survives the catastrophe, setting the stage for future conflicts.

| ANDROIDS SAGA |

The Android Saga begins when Future Trunks, the son of Vegeta and Bulma from an alternate timeline, arrives to warn the Z Fighters about the coming threat of powerful Androids created by Dr. Gero, a vengeful scientist from the Red Ribbon Army. Despite their training, the Z Fighters are soon overwhelmed by Android 19 and Android 20 (Dr. Gero himself), but Vegeta reveals his new Super Saiyan form and destroys Android 19. Later, the even more dangerous Androids 17 and 18 are activated, easily defeating the heroes. Meanwhile, a mysterious bio-engineered creature named Cell emerges, absorbing other lifeforms to reach his perfect form. As Cell grows stronger, the Z Fighters desperately train in the Hyperbolic Time Chamber. After achieving his perfect state by absorbing Android 17 and Android 18, Cell announces the Cell Games, a tournament to decide the fate of the Earth. In the final confrontation, Gohan ascends to Super Saiyan 2, unleashing his hidden power to destroy Cell once and for all, though Goku sacrifices himself to save the planet during the battle.

| MAJIN BUU SAGA |

The Majin Buu Saga begins when the evil wizard Babidi arrives on Earth to resurrect Majin Buu, an ancient and incredibly powerful creature of destruction. Babidi manipulates and corrupts fighters like Vegeta, who allows himself to be possessed to gain the strength to fight Goku. Despite Goku and Vegeta's fierce battle, Buu is eventually awakened and proves unstoppable, defeating many of the Z Fighters with ease. After Buu kills Babidi and goes on a rampage, he splits into different forms, each more dangerous than the last, including the sinister Super Buu and the childlike but terrifying Kid Buu. Goku and Vegeta fuse into Vegito to combat Buu but ultimately separate again to save their absorbed friends. In the final showdown, Goku gathers energy from across the universe to form a massive Spirit Bomb. With the help of the Dragon Balls and the revived people of Earth, Goku destroys Kid Buu, bringing peace back to the world. The saga ends as Goku meets Uub, the good reincarnation of Buu, whom he decides to train as the next protector of Earth.
